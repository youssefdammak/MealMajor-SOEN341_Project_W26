### Summary of Discussion  

We discussed the new task breakdown for the upcoming sprint. The priority of the meeting was determining what our unique feature was
going to be. Some suggestions included a virtual fridge to organize ingredients, a grocery list to plan meal purchases, and a social
media-type platform to allow meals to be reviewed. We discarded the review idea as another team planned something similar and chose to expand upon our fridge idea. We decided to also include a feature that allows you to select items from the virtual fridge and get meal
recommendations based on the selection. We discussed what components would be required for this implementation, including UI for the fridge and functions to filter through lists of corresponding recipes.
